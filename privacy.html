Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the content-automation application "korean-shorts-poster" (the "App"), operated by the owner of the TikTok account @my_k_soul and the YouTube channel "Almost Everything in Korea" (the "Operator", "we", "us"), handles information in connection with its use of the TikTok APIs.

1. Scope

The App is a private automation tool used solely by the Operator to publish the Operator's own original short-form Korean-language educational videos to the Operator's own TikTok account. The App does not serve, onboard, or process data for any other end users.

2. Information We Access

  • TikTok account identity (via the user.info.basic scope): the Operator's open ID and display name, used only to confirm the authenticated account before publishing.
  • Authorization tokens: OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok, used to upload and publish the Operator's own videos.
  • The Operator's own video files and captions, which the Operator creates and submits for posting.

We do not collect, access, or store personal data belonging to any other TikTok users.

3. How We Use Information

Accessed information is used exclusively to authenticate the Operator's account and to upload and publish the Operator's own videos to that account on an automated schedule. We do not use the information for advertising, profiling, or any unrelated purpose.

4. Storage & Security

OAuth tokens are stored on the Operator's private server with restricted file permissions and are used only for server-to-TikTok API calls over HTTPS. Tokens are refreshed automatically and are never shared.

5. Sharing & Selling

We do not sell, rent, or share any accessed information with third parties. Data is transmitted only between the Operator's server and TikTok's official API endpoints.

6. Data Retention

Tokens are retained only while the App is active and are deleted upon revocation of access in the TikTok account settings or developer portal.

7. Your Controls

The Operator may revoke the App's access at any time via TikTok's "Manage app permissions" settings, which immediately invalidates the stored tokens.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to chanwook.moon@gmail.com.
